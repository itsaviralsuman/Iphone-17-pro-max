01 / Design

Unibody enclosure.
Makes a strong case for itself.

Designed from the inside out. A heat-forged aluminum unibody maximizes performance, battery capacity, and durability — and cradles a laser-welded vapor chamber that keeps the A19 Pro running hard for longer.

Three bold finishes.

An anodized aluminum unibody in deep, heat-forged tones. Shown in Cosmic Orange.

Vapor chamber

Deionized water sealed inside moves heat away from the A19 Pro — unlocking higher sustained performance.

Immersive pro display

6.3″ and 6.9″ Super Retina XDR. Brighter, with better anti-reflection and ProMotion up to 120Hz.

Ceramic Shield 2

4× more crack-resistant on the back; new Ceramic Shield 2 on the front with 3× better scratch resistance.

Camera Control

Take a photo, record video, and dial in settings with a touch-sensitive button — so you never miss a moment.

A big zoom forward.

All three rear cameras are 48MP. The telephoto uses the next-generation tetraprism design with a 56% larger sensor — an equivalent 200mm focal length and 8x optical-quality zoom. The longest iPhone telephoto ever, with a 16x total optical zoom range.
